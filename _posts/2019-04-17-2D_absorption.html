--- title: Add absorption layer to the FSMDM ---


Adding absorption layer can help reduce the spatial aliasing error. But if the absorption is too strong, the acoustic field will become inaccurate due to excess absoprtion. On the other hand, if the absoprtion is too small, then the aliasing error cannot be reduced. In this case, the effect of absorption layer is investigated by setting up different medium.absorb_gamma and medium.absorb_alpha.

2D forward simulation

This simulation setup is based on the Simulation in the 2D homogeneous media using the frequency-specific mixed domain method. Computational domain and simulation setup are the same in the two cases. In this case, three pairs of medium.absorb_gamma and medium.absorb_alpha are studied and they are: medium.absorb_gamma = 0.1 and medium.absorb_alpha=0.5, medium.absorb_gamma = 0.5 and medium.absorb_alpha=0.2, and medium.absorb_gamma = 0.8 and medium.absorb_alpha=0.08. The pressure distribution along the central axia simulated at different absorption layer configurations are shown in the figure below. For each simulation case, coefficients for the absorption layer should be selected carefully.
